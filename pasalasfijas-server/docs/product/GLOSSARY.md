# Glossary

## Pick

A specific recommended selection for a betting market.

## Main pick

The primary recommendation for a match, if value and risk are acceptable.

## Alternative pick

A secondary recommendation with lower priority.

## ComboBet

A suggested combined selection made from multiple legs.

## NO_BET

A deliberate decision not to recommend a bet because data, risk or value is not favorable.

## RiskScore

0-100 score where higher means more risk.

## ValueScore

0-100 score where higher means better perceived betting value.

## Stake index

0-100 confidence-based stake indicator. It is not a direct percentage of the user's bankroll.
